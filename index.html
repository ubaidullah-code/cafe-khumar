Our philosophy

Cuisine as
celestial art

Stellar Heights was conceived as a canvas for nature's finest ingredients, guided by a philosophy that every plate should evoke wonder. Our team of award-winning chefs sources rare produce from across continents, crafting tasting experiences that are as visually transcendent as the panorama that surrounds you.

Perched 43 floors above the city, the restaurant offers an unobstructed panoramic view — a backdrop that changes with the seasons, the hour, the mood of the sky.
